Welcome to my personal website where I share updates, reflections, and blogs on the books I read and ideas that inspire me. My name is Xingcai, though many of you might have known me as Saber Yu. I grew up in Guangzhou, China, and moved to Canada when I was 15. Since then, I have studied, worked, and built a family here. Today, I am a father of two, a researcher and a computer vision engineer. I hope you find my blogs interesting in that they provide unique perspectives about things (and it's totally OKAY that you don't agree with my ideas). Feel free to reach out to me on or anytime.
